Parametric topology optimization

Summary

A performance-driven workflow combining parametric modeling with topology optimization (BESO) for high-rise structural design under wind and gravity loads.

Optimization Structures Parametric

Overview

The goal is to explore structural systems efficiently by linking parametric inputs to an optimization loop. Wind and gravity loads guide material distribution and structural form exploration.
